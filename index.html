About Us

Company Overview

Kibe Contractors Limited is a construction and contracting company specializing in delivering high-quality construction services across various sectors including residential, commercial, industrial, and roads. We are committed to excellence and customer satisfaction, and have built a reputation for reliability, innovation, and integrity.

History

Established in 2014, Kibe Contractors Limited began as a small business with a vision to provide top-notch construction services. Growth has been a norm in our company; On 8th September, the same year (2014), the company was incorporated and it became Limited. Over the years, we have grown significantly, expanding our service offerings and project capabilities. Our success is built on hard work, dedication, and a strong focus on quality and client satisfaction.
